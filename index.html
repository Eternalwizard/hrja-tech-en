EPA 30B Sampling Tubes - Tianjin Huaruijiaan Technology Co., Ltd.

EPA 30B Sampling Tubes

Sampling method: 30B mercury adsorption tube is a special sampling tube for method 30B, which meets the requirements of US EPA method 30B. It is divided into three types: total mercury adsorption tube, elemental mercury adsorption tube, and spiked adsorption tube. It uses modified activated carbon to be loaded and installed at the front end of the heated sampling probe of the mercury sampling instrument to adsorb and capture gaseous mercury in the flue gas. The 30B mercury adsorption tube is used for flue gas mercury sampling from fixed pollution sources.

Sampling tube parameters: 185*10 mm;

Technical features:

  • Two-section adsorption tube, filled with modified coconut shell activated carbon, and separated by quartz fiber cotton
  • Modified activated carbon has extremely low mercury content, large mercury adsorption capacity, and high collection efficiency, making it difficult to penetrate due to high mercury background
  • Each tube has a unique number, making it easy to control the sampling and analysis process
  • Supports customized spiked adsorption tubes with a spiking error within Â±3%
