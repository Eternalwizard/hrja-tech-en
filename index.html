About Us

Tianjin Huaruijiaan Technology Co., Ltd. is a high-tech enterprise focusing on environmental protection technology research and application. We are dedicated to providing efficient and accurate environmental monitoring solutions. Our main products include EPA 30B sampling tubes and various mercury testing services. Our products and services are widely used in fields such as fixed pollution source flue gas mercury sampling and aerosol particle size testing, ensuring that customers obtain accurate and reliable environmental monitoring data. The company has a professional R&D team and advanced laboratory equipment, always adhering to technological innovation as the core driving force to continuously improve product quality and service levels. We adopt internationally standardized testing methods and technologies to provide customers with comprehensive environmental monitoring solutions, including the determination of total mercury in environmental air, soil, water quality, and solid waste, as well as aerosol particle size distribution. Huaruijiaan Technology always upholds the corporate philosophy of "integrity, innovation, cooperation, and win-win" and is committed to promoting the development and progress of environmental monitoring technology. We will continue to work hard to provide better products and services to our customers, contributing to environmental protection and building a better home together.
